Freedom Loader

Put freedom in your downloads

About • Download • Wiki

Freedom Loader is a lightweight desktop app (Electron) that wraps yt-dlp to give users an easy, reliable way to download online video and audio. It focuses on three things: control, privacy, and simplicity. Freedom Loader ships with an integrated yt-dlp binary, optional ffmpeg support for merging/encoding, and tools to handle site-specific quirks like authenticated content via browser cookies.
