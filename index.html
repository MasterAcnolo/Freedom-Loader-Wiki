About Freedom Loader

Freedom Loader is a lightweight desktop app that uses yt-dlp to give users a simple, reliable way to download online video and audio. Its focus: control, privacy, and simplicity.

I built this project because I was tired of download tools filled with ads, limits, and paywalls. Frustrated by seeing everything driven by profit instead of usefulness, Freedom Loader exists to put control back in the user's hands.

Basically, I just wanted to create the tool I wish I had when I was younger: easy, reliable offline access to the content you want, without any hassle.
